The Visitor
Chong Hyon-Jong
A visitor comes.
It is a tremendous feat.
I open my door,
And make a hospitable place.

The visitor comes
With his past and present and his future.
Like the wind that broke through my window,
He comes and goes as he pleases.

The visitor comes
With a heart that has been broken.
Can my heart mimic that wind
And become a hospitable place?
